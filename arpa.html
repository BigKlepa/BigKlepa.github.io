ARPA

(due to recent NDA updates, I've removed the video of this project)

I was employed at FIT Turku, Turku AMK, where I worked for the ARPA project. My work at ARPA has primarily focused on creating a digital twin for an armature robot. It's the largest project I've worked on, in terms of scope, and people involved. I've learned a lot about version control, and I've been introduced to many new technologies, such as WebXR and MQTT, and the challenges that come with them.

Technologies and Tools used: Unity, C#, VS, Blender, GitHub, BitBucket, OpenXR, WebXR, MQTT, WebGL

You can read more about ARPA at their website
